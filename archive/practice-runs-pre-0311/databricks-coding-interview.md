Candidate Prep: Solutions Architect Coding Interview

Welcome to the next stage of the Databricks interview process! This 60-minute session is designed to mirror the actual
day-to-day life of a Solutions Architect (SA). In the field, SAs are often asked to reproduce errors or help customers navigate
complex issues—frequently without having direct access to their underlying data. This interview reflects that reality, focusing
on your ability to troubleshoot and build solutions under those same real-world constraints.

't looking for perfect syntax memorization; we are looking for first principles thinkers with an AI-builder mindset and
a deep intrinsic curiosity.

Phase 1: Discovery
The interviewer will provide a prompt that you will use to generate a dataset. Ask clarifying questions to define the scope. Pro
Tip: Feel free to type your AI prompts in a separate scratchpad to iterate on them before pasting them into the Databricks
notebook.
Phase 2: Building & Problem Solving
Generate a dataset using AI tools. You may use the Databricks Assistant, or your favorite third party tool. We just ask that you
share your full screen, so we can see how you approach the problem, and how you leverage your tools. You will then build a data
pipeline or analytical solution based on the requirements defined.
●
You will need to generate a synthetic dataset to work with.
●
AI as a Partner: You are encouraged to use AI to scaffold code. However, you are the pilot. You must audit the AI’s
output. If it "hallucinates
"

●
Distributed Reasoning: We will discuss how your code executes. Be prepared to talk about how your code scales.
4. Evaluation Criteria: How We Measure Success
We are assessing three main signals:
1. 2. 3. Computational Thinking: Can you break a messy problem into a logical sequence of transformations?
Code Stewardship: Can you generate working code and—more importantly—can you explain exactly what it’s doing
under the hood?
Resilience: If you hit a bug or a logic error, how do you handle it? We don
't mind if you need to work through it; we care
about how you navigate to a solution.
