Job Description
Wegmans is looking to expand its team of Solution Architects. As they continue to modernize their environment, the need to bring in professionals that have experience with Data, AI, and Cloud is paramount.  As a Solutions Architect, you’ll design scalable solutions, partner with engineering teams, and ensure alignment with enterprise strategy. You’ll work closely with stakeholders to turn business needs into technical blueprints, evaluate emerging technologies, and deliver Proofs of Concept. As a Solutions Architect focused on data, you will use Databricks to design and improve data architecture for advanced analytics and machine learning across the organization. Your role involves setting architectural standards, guiding implementation, and promoting continuous improvement while staying ahead of industry trends, with a focus on modern cloud data platforms and best practices for data engineering and analytics.
