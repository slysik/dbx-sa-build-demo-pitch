Multi-region: One metastore per region; Delta Sharing for cross-region; row filters by country tags for data residency
Databricks vs Snowflake
Price/Performance: 2.8x faster, 3.6x less cost (TPC-DS-like)
Streaming: Native Structured Streaming, DLT, Auto Loader vs Snowpipe
AI/ML: Native MLflow, Feature Store, Model Serving (more mature)
Open Formats: Delta Lake, zero vendor lock-in vs Iceberg recently added
Governance: Unity Catalog (multi-cloud, multi-language) vs SQL-level
Ease of use: Steeper curve but more powerful

Diplomatic framing: "Both have strengths. Databricks differentiates on the unified platform — DE, DW, and ML on one platform with open formats. For customers doing more than just BI, the TCO advantage is significant because you eliminate the integration tax."
